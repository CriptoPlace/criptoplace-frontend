Inspired by Reddit's Project Place, the crypto-oplace is a platform for co-creation of the entire blockchain community. The project has a similar idea - it's an infinite (notional) canvas that anyone can contribute to. anyone! The whole canvas is divided into 16x16 pixels tiles. Add one or more pictures To create a group art created by the crypto community.


CryptoPlus does not aim to make millions or rob anyone. Most of the smart contract features are free and the prices are rather symbolic. We just like to come up with new uses for blockchain technology and exploring the possibilities it presents to us.

Project Features

  • The project is 100% blockchain-based! We don't use servers or IPFS to store images they are completely stored in the blockchain forever.
  • The entire source code of the smartcontract, the backend and the frontend parts are in an open repository and can clone by anyone who wants to. If you wish, you can deploy the project locally on your computer - the data is in the blockchain and cannot be controlled by third parties.
  • Our contract is deployed on all popular blockchains and each builds its own unique art!
  • Initially, each tile is "free" and can be changed for free by any member of the community for free (not counting the price of gas).
  • After you fill 999 tiles, you can buy tiles. The initial price of each tile is calculated by the formula. The closer to the center, the more expensive!

CryptoPlace for everyone!

Of course, the most valuable tiles in the center of the art will be occupied and quite expensive, but the field is huge. No one is stopping you from occupying a small section of canvas on the outskirts and create a small postcard for a friend or loved one. By taking ownership of the tiles you can be sure that the picture will stay in the blockchain forever!

Project Roadmap

The project is conditionally divided into three stages:

  • The first stage is the most peaceful! The only function available filling in the tiles. Anyone may fill in any crayon he likes! The first stage ends after 999 tiles have been filled (including repeated fills of the same tiles).
  • After the 999th invocation of the Fill Tile function, the project enters the second stage. Users have the opportunity to buy the ownership of the tiles. This is not a complete project yet, but now only you can modify purchased by you. While the number of modified tiles (including re-changes) reaches 4999 and the number of purchased tiles reaches reaches 4999 the second stage will continue.
  • The third stage begins when both conditions are met - the number of filled The number of tiles reaches 4999 and the number of purchased tiles reaches 499. At this point the project starts in full force! Tiles can buy, transfer, put up for sale, sold through the auction, rent them out. Game on!

Pricing

Initially, the price of each tile is fixed. The central tile costs 1 ETH (or another coin, depending on the blockchain on which the smart contract is deployed). The 4 tiles around the central one cost 0.25 ETH each. The 8 tiles of the next circle are worth 0.125 ETH each. The basic idea is that all tiles of one circle costs a total of 1 ETH. But at the same time a tile cannot cost less than 0.001 ETH. Or if we speak in formula language:
price = MAX(1 / MAX(1, (ABS(x) + ABS(y)) * 4), 0.001)

NFT

Although the idea of cryptocurrency is much broader than the NFT concept, it supports the ESR-721 standard. Our NFT is not mintable in the classic sense of the word. After the project moves to the second stage, users can buy tiles into ownership. After a user has bought a tile to own. After that, the cache becomes an NFT. The X and Y coordinates of the cache are converted into a unique identifier (unsigned). Therefore, not every cache can become NFT, but only those in the center. In total half of all tiles can be converted to NFT.
